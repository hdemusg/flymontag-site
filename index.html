About Fly Montag

Fly Montag is a musician based in New York, NY. Growing up in the Atlanta area in an Indian-American household, he learned how to play the piano and double bass, and was exposed to singing through bhajans (Hindu devotional songs) and his sisters' Carnatic singing lessons. While in high school, he decided to start learning how to produce electronic dance music (EDM), and decided to start DJing as a college freshman at Georgia Tech.

For the past 6 years, he has been working on his craft in both the production and DJing spaces. As a producer, he has released 2 EPs and 1 album, and runs his own imprint, named 451 Records.

As a DJ, he has performed at shows around the United States, including in Atlanta, Seattle, and New York, and opened for Kid Koala and Blackbear during his time as a college student.

Fly Montag is currently working on his second studio album, on which he also sings and raps, and is hoping to share it with the world in 2025.
